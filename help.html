Frequently asked questions

Search for a city from the dashboard, open its weather result, then select the star button. The city will appear on the Favorites page. Select the star again to remove it.

Icons summarize the expected condition for that time period: clear sky, cloud cover, rain, thunderstorms, snow, mist, or other atmospheric conditions. The text description beside each icon provides the precise condition.

WeatherScope displays the AQI value supplied by its weather data service and groups it into an easy-to-read health category. Pollutant details may include PM2.5, PM10, nitrogen dioxide, ozone, and related measurements when available.

Use the °C / °F unit control in the dashboard header. WeatherScope remembers your selection in the browser for future visits.

Confirm that location access is enabled for the browser and WeatherScope. Reload the page after changing permission. You can always search for your city manually if GPS is unavailable.

Check your connection, refresh the page, and disable restrictive content blockers for the site. If the problem continues, clear the browser cache and reopen WeatherScope.
